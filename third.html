About the company

We are glad to welcome you on our company's website! "HAPPY CAKE" is a production organization specializing in the food industry and has been successfully operating in Almaty since 2005. The main activity of the company is the manufacture of high-quality confectionery and bakery products, culinary products and frozen semi-finished products. Production workshops are equipped with modern equipment, sanitary norms and rules are strictly observed. All products are made from the best natural ingredients and are handmade. Each product passes through caring hands and according to a carefully selected recipe. The company introduces innovations, but at the same time values traditions, combining them with advanced technologies. In addition, the company is successfully engaged in an off-site buffet and catering. Now the company has 6 stores in different parts of the city, including in the region.
