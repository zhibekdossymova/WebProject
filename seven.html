Chefs

Julie Tan

With over 25 years of experience, Chef Julie Tan is one of the most experienced Certified Master Tasters on the panel. Although trained in the art of French cooking, Julie works closely with Chef Martin Yan developing recipes, writing cookbooks, and serving as culinary coordinator for Yan Can Cook productions.

David Herman

David Jarrett Herman was born February 12th, 1969 in New Jersey to parents who owned a Specialty Food Distribution Company. Because of this, at an early age, David was exposed to many International cuisines.

Christina Cutino

Chef Christina Cutino has been in the food and beverage industry for the past 25 years, of which 14 years have been spent as owner and operator of Cutinos Catering in San Francisco.

James Reid

Chef James Reid is a graduate from Hastings College in England, where he studied hotel and restaurant management. He has worked in fine restaurants throughout England, France and Germany.

Adriano Zumbo

Adriano Zumbo is no ordinary pastry chef. His creations are admired by critics and the public. And for good reason - they are unique in their concept and execution - simply put, you will not find anything better and tastier in all of Almaty.

JJulien Alvarez

World Champion in Confectionery. For 3 years he taught at the gastronomy school Bellouet Conseil Paris.

Jerome De Oliveira

Jerome De Oliveira is the youngest world champion, twice. Jerome received his first title at the age of 23. Today he is 30.

Amaury Guichon

Amaury Guichon is a young and talented culinary specialist at Jean Philippe Patisserie in Las Vegas who knows how to make chocolate taste as good as it looks great.
